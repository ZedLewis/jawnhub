About

As an avid clothes enthusiast who like many has spent too much money on his hobby over the years, I recently started tracking my incomings and outgoings out of curiosity. This spurned the Jawnhub idea, a project I could use to document such things and could be expanded as a hub of information. Think Letterboxd but for likeminded folk who are into how they dress.

"Jawn"

Philly slang for a person, place, or thing

Rather than keep all of this stuff tucked away in some boring old spreadsheet, why not have some fun and build a little application to make things pretty.
